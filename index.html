Overview

You are a soccer enthusiast? Are you having trouble finding your playground and teammates? SportsMates will be an excellent solution for you. This software enables you to search for soccer matches, find teammates and book online. Saving you time and convenience in finding your playground. Sportsmates also assists field owners with field management. Finding your field will be easier because SportsMates will help you find fields near your location. SportsMates also has other features is allowing you to create your own matches and find new players. Additionally, SportsMate will let you know which matches are missing players so you can join them.

Motivation

When I was in high school, I played soccer a lot, it was up to 3 to 4 times per week. The problem we often faced was that all the soccer fields were full and we did not know which ones were available at that time. It took us a while to check each field individually. We had difficulty getting a field to play on because it took so much time and effort. SportsMates was created for this reason. Soccer is considered the king sport among sports. According to the data recorded in 2021, there were 3.4 billion people in the world who knew and followed soccer and more than a billion players. I conducted a survey about the problems and difficulties of football players 2 months ago. There were 126 people who responded and nearly 50% of those people had inconveniences about placing a football field. Through the above data, I have confidence that my project is a potential one and is supported by everyone.

Description

Our application will focus development on two main sections. The first one is the searching and booking of soccer field sections for users. Next, we will provide the soccer field owners with a customized application to be able to receive the user's booking request. In addition, this applicationwill helps soccer field owners manage and arrange their fields. This can help them optimize the rental time of their fields, which can minimize the problem of vacant fields during peak hours. Fields owners often have problems with players booking by mobile phone but not coming to play at the booked time. This affects the owner of the soccer field who cannot arrange and optimize their fields. When booking through our application, users will have to make a deposit of at least 30% via online payment applications. Thus, the owners will feel more secure when receiving the deposit and personal information of the user. Our application will have a cancellation feature for users in case of an unforeseen situation. The user will have to cancel 2 hours before the start of the rental, and the deposit will be refunded to the user. In case the user cancels after 2 hours before the rental time, the user cannot get the deposit back. Each user will have 100 moral points. If a user deliberately books a field and does not arrive, we will have warnings and penalties. These people will have their moral points reduced and if their score is under 50, they will be banned from booking or hosting matches.The software will be integrated with the map so users will be able to easily see the empty fields around them. When the user presses on the field they are interested in. All information about that field will be displayed, including course name, address, phone number, field type (fields for 5 players, fields for 7 players, or field for 11 players), and available hours. Users can also see the matches that are lacking people to participate in. Users can also rate and give feedback about the quality and service of that football field after using it. Fields with the highest rating will be suggested on our software. SportsMaster also has a filter to help users easily find a suitable field for them. This filter helps users to filter information such as location, price, field type, and field with available time that the user looking. In our application, we always suggest the field that is closest to the user's location, saving users time in finding the field. In order to ensure user quality, users must fill in their full names, phone numbers, email addresses, and ID cards(this can be car license, passport, student card or identify card) when registering an account. The SportsMates online field booking application offers quick and easy use, along with a variety of information about soccer fields, helping users no longer have to worry about the quality and saving time in looking for a field that fits their needs.

Skill Required

In order to successfully develop and launch SportsMates, various knowledge and skills across different areas are needed. In the first place, I will use AWS services with best practices for backend computing, and databases to optimize resiliency, robustness, performance and availability. The services are AWS Lambda, AWS DynamoDB, AWS API Gateway, and S3 to name a few. They will be utilized to create our infrastructure via CDK (Cloud Development Kit) which allows me to easily develop, test, maintain and deploy it to AWS as code. In addition, User Experience (UX) and User Interface (UI) design are crucial skills when designing an application's interface. A cybersecurity skill is necessary because it helps secure information and ensures that mobile applications are safe to use. Aside from technology knowledge, we also need business skills to ensure that the application is able to operate sustainably. For example, Marketing skills can be used to advertise a project and promotional strategies can be used to obtain users.

Technologies

First and foremost, I want to be able to quickly build a Minimal Viable Product (MVP) to fish for your feedback on main features; therefore, practical knowledge of modern web technologies and programming languages is needed. Specifically, they are Typescript, NextJs, NextUI, React Query, and Playwright for front-end development and testing. In addition to that, it's critical for this app to be mobile-friendly and accessible. Therefore, PWA (Progressive Web App) technology will be used in the early stage to save time on development and release. Swift and Kotlin are needed in a later stage to develop native mobile apps. These modern technologies offer out-of-the-box best frontend practices for developer experiences that allow me to focus on building what matters most to the users, user-friendly features that solve users' problems. In order to iterate quickly on my product, it's crucial for my development workflow to have effective and reliable CI/CD pipelines. For the front-end, skills in GitHub Actions, AWS Codebuild, and bash scripting are required for this project.

Outcome

In the event that SportsMates performs as expected and is supported by people and society. There will be widespread recognition and influence for SportsMates on the market. There will be an appreciation for SportsMates among the public as an app that makes placing soccer fields easier. A number of previously difficult problems will be solved at SportsMates, including the inability to book online, the inconvenience of finding an empty field, and especially helping individuals who can find matches to join and make new teammates. By developing the SportsMates app, it will be possible to increase people's desire to play soccer.
